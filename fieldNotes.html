Field Notes

Photographs made without an assignment. Desert towns, empty rooms, street noise, and the small details that stick—sometimes for reasons I can’t explain.

Field Notes is where the work slows down. These photographs come from time spent driving, walking, waiting, and looking—often alone. Abandoned homes, desert towns, protests, streets, objects that caught my attention for reasons I didn’t fully understand at the time.

Some of this work never becomes anything else. That’s the point.
