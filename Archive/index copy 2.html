About Ric

If we’re going to make pictures, we should probably vibe.

I’m Ric — photographer, desert enthusiast, and person who will absolutely pull the car over if the light looks interesting.


My work lives somewhere between fashion and field notes: clean compositions with a little dust on them, people who look like themselves (just slightly more iconic), and locations that feel like characters.


I’m based in Los Angeles but happily chase good light to the edges of California and beyond. If you like the idea of your shoot feeling more like a weird little adventure than a chore, we’ll get along fine.

Currently into: Trona Pinnacles at blue hour, film scans that aren’t “perfect,” and portraits that look like a scene you want to pause and stare at.
Services
Editorial & lookbooks · Portrait sessions · Branding campaigns · On-location shoots · Select video work with collaborators
Clients & collaborators
